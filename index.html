Deiadora has been transforming audiences with her voice her entire life.

From high school and collegiate speech and debate to decades of professional speaking, facilitation, and teaching — Deiadora brings the full bandwidth of a natural technologist, researcher, writer, and musician to every room she enters. The framework she speaks from is the same one she has been researching and refining for thirteen years: the practical architecture of a coherent life that actually sustains you, the planet, and the future of tech.

Her upcoming book, Quantum Currency: Field Research on Human Coherence, is the formal statement of that research. The talk is where it becomes a lived experience in the room.
